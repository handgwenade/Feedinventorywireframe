## Short Description

StockLog is a mobile-first inventory and invoicing app for feed and ranch operations. Track feed/mineral stock, customer sales, K2/internal use, invoices, payments, reports, and team access with organization-scoped cloud data.

## Long Description

StockLog helps feed and ranch operations manage everyday inventory and invoicing workflows from a mobile-first app.

Use StockLog to track feed and mineral products, current quantities, low-stock needs, customer accounts, K2/internal account use, invoices, payments, and operational reports. Team members can sign in with email/password, join by invite, and access workflows based on their assigned role.

Current beta-ready workflows include:

- Email/password login.
- New ranch/organization setup.
- User invitations and invite acceptance.
- Manage Users for active users and pending invites.
- Product and inventory views.
- Add stock and adjust counts.
- Customer feed sales.
- K2/internal feed use.
- Invoice and payment records.
- Inventory, sales, K2, payment, and unpaid invoice reports.
- Organization-scoped Supabase-backed data.

StockLog is designed for practical feed/ranch recordkeeping. During beta, users should continue keeping backup records for critical business activity and report issues to support.

## Data Collection Disclosure Draft

Final App Privacy answers should be reviewed against the live app, Supabase configuration, and any analytics/logging tools enabled before submission.

Data that may be collected:

- Account info: user name, email, profile, role, status.
- User content/business data: organization/ranch details, products, inventory activity, customer/K2 accounts, phone numbers, emails, addresses, invoices, payments, notes, reports, and invite records.
- Identifiers/authentication: Supabase user IDs, organization IDs, session/authentication identifiers.
- Diagnostics/logs, if applicable: error logs, security logs, device/browser metadata, timestamps, and troubleshooting details.

Draft disclosure notes:

- Data is used to provide app functionality, authentication, support, security, reporting, and business recordkeeping.
- Data is organization-scoped for authorized users.
- StockLog does not intentionally collect payment card numbers.
- StockLog does not sell user data.
- Private exports are local/admin-controlled and should not be committed or shared publicly.
