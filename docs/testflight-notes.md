# StockLog TestFlight Notes

Use this draft for TestFlight beta tester instructions.

## Beta Tester Welcome Note

Welcome to the StockLog beta.

StockLog is a mobile-first inventory and invoicing app for feed and ranch operations. This beta is focused on testing everyday workflows: signing in, setting up or joining a ranch, managing users, tracking products and inventory, recording customer feed sales, recording K2/internal use, reviewing invoices/payments, and checking reports.

This is a beta build. Please keep backup business records while testing.

## What to Test

Please test the workflows that match your role:

- Email/password login and logout.
- Invite acceptance and first login.
- Set up a new ranch/organization if asked.
- Manage Users, active users, pending invites, and role access.
- Product and inventory lists.
- Product detail pages.
- Add stock.
- Adjust count.
- Customer sale / Take Feed.
- K2/internal use.
- Invoice detail.
- Print/PDF if available on your device.
- Record payment.
- Reports: inventory summary, low stock, customer sales, K2 use, payments received, unpaid invoices.
- Reloading/reopening the app and confirming data is still present.
- iPhone layout: safe-area headers, bottom navigation, back buttons, profile icon, and keyboard behavior.

## Known Limitations

- This beta should be treated as a recordkeeping aid, not the only business record source.
- Email display for active users may be limited depending on available profile data.
- Invite codes are shown once and should be copied/sent securely when created.
- Historical import/export tools are admin-controlled and local/private.
- Some settings may not be fully cloud-backed yet and should be verified before relying on them.
- Family/person flows may remain disabled if not part of the current beta scope.
- Advanced accounting integrations, online card payments, offline mode, and full historical migration workflows are not part of the current beta.

## What to Include in Bug Reports

Please include:

- Your account email.
- Ranch/organization name.
- Your role, if known.
- Device model and iOS version.
- StockLog build/version.
- Screen or action where the issue happened.
- What happened.
- What you expected to happen.
- Whether you can reproduce it.
- Screenshot or screen recording, if possible.
- Approximate date/time of the issue.

Do not send passwords, service-role keys, private export files, or live invite codes in bug reports.

## Backup Records During Beta

During beta, keep backup records for critical inventory, customer sales, invoices, payments, and K2/internal use. If StockLog is unavailable or a record looks incorrect, continue operations using backup records and report the issue.
