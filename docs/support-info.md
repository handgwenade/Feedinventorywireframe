## What to Include When Requesting Support

To help troubleshoot quickly, include:

- Account email used to sign in.
- Ranch/organization name.
- Screen or action where the issue happened.
- What happened.
- What you expected to happen.
- Screenshot or screen recording, if possible.
- Approximate date/time of the issue.
- Device model and iOS version, if using the mobile app.

Do not send passwords, one-time invite codes, service-role keys, private export files, or other secrets through normal support channels.

## Emergency and Business Continuity Note

StockLog is a recordkeeping tool. During beta, users should keep backup records for critical inventory, invoice, payment, and customer activity. If StockLog is unavailable or a record looks incorrect, continue business operations using backup records and contact support when practical.

## Beta Known Limitations

- Email display for active users may be limited depending on the user/profile data available to the app.
- Invite codes are shown once during onboarding and should be copied/sent securely at that time.
- Historical import/export tools are local/admin-controlled and should be handled as private operational data.
- Some settings may not be fully cloud-backed yet and should be verified before wider release.
- Family/person flows may remain disabled for beta if not part of the current launch scope.
