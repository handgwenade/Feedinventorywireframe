What to Include When Requesting Support

To help troubleshoot quickly, include:

  • Account email used to sign in.
  • Ranch/organization name.
  • Screen or action where the issue happened.
  • What happened.
  • What you expected to happen.
  • Screenshot or screen recording, if possible.
  • Approximate date/time of the issue.
  • Device model and iOS version, if using the mobile app.

Do not send passwords, one-time invite codes, service-role keys, private export files, or other secrets through normal support channels.

Business Continuity Note

StockLog is a recordkeeping tool. During beta or early release use, users should keep backup records for critical inventory, invoice, payment, and customer activity. If StockLog is unavailable or a record looks incorrect, continue business operations using backup records and contact support when practical.
